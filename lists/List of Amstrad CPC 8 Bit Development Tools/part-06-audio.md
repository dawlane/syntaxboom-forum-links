**Overview**  
Arkos Tracker is one of the most widely used music composition tools in the Amstrad CPC development community. It is a specialised tracker designed for creating music and sound effects for systems based on the General Instrument AY-3-8910 and AY-3-8912 programmable sound generators used throughout the CPC family.

The software provides a modern cross-platform composition environment while targeting the strict memory and performance limitations of original 8-bit hardware. Musicians and developers can compose complete game soundtracks, demo music, menu themes, jingles, and sound effects using tracker-based workflows that remain familiar to retro-computing composers.

Arkos Tracker is especially popular because it is accompanied by efficient replay routines designed for use on real hardware. These playback engines allow compositions to be integrated into games and demos while consuming relatively little CPU time and memory.

The tool has become a de facto standard within the modern CPC scene and is frequently used in commercial homebrew releases, demoscene productions, educational projects, and hobby software. Its combination of ease of use, high-quality output, and efficient runtime integration makes it one of the most important audio development tools available for the platform.
